Our Story

Daina and Bradley met in November of 2022, and started dating in February 2023. Both being designers, they instantly had effortless conversations with each other, spending their 1st date talking so much that they walked from Olympic Park to Rhodes without any awkward silence.

They have kept that spark alive in their relationship since then, going on adventures together and looking after each other when the other needed it.

Brad had to spin quite the story to get Daina back to Sydney Olympic Park without fully realising what was happening. They were “going for afternoon tea in a park in Concord West”. And on the 30th of August, Bradley proposed and Daina said yes.
